Avengers

Earth's mightiest heroes, united for one purpose — saving the world

Iron Man

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist. Tony Stark built the Iron Man suit to escape captivity, then turned it into a symbol of protection. He’s the brains behind most of the Avengers’ tech. Witty, sarcastic, but deeply selfless at heart. He may hide behind armor, but he’s all heart underneath. "I am Iron Man."

Thor

The God of Thunder and prince of Asgard. Thor wields the mighty hammer Mjölnir and controls lightning. Once arrogant, now a humbled protector of Earth and the realms. He’s powerful, but driven by honor and loyalty. A warrior with the heart of a hero — and a love for beer. "Bring me Thanos""

Captain America

Steve Rogers — a kid from Brooklyn turned super soldier. He stands for justice, integrity, and doing what’s right. Wields the iconic vibranium shield like no other. He led the Avengers with unwavering courage. Even time couldn't change his values. "I can do this all day.""

Spider Man

The friendly neighborhood hero from Queens. Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gained agility, strength, and web-slinging powers. He juggles teenage life with saving the city. Smart, awkward, brave — with a heart full of responsibility. Always trying to live up to Tony’s legacy. "With great power comes great responsibility.""
